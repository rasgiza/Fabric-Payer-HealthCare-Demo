# AMA Prior Authorization Survey

Source: American Medical Association annual prior-authorization physician survey [CIT:AMA-PA-SURVEY-2024].

## Headline findings

- **94%** of physicians report PA causes care delays
- **78%** report PA leading patients to abandon recommended treatment
- **24%** of physicians report PA-related serious adverse event for at least one patient
- Average **42 PAs per physician per week**
- Average **12 hours per week** of physician/staff time on PA
- **93%** of physicians say PA negatively impacts clinical outcomes
- Peer-to-peer overturn rate **~39%** when challenged

## PA volume drivers (top services)

1. Imaging - high-cost (MRI, CT, PET)
2. Specialty drugs - injectables, biologics
3. DME - power wheelchairs, CPAP, complex orthotics
4. Genetic testing
5. Inpatient admission (concurrent review)
6. Behavioral health residential

## Cost per PA decision

Industry studies cite $25-$93 per-decision payer cost. Combined with provider-side cost (~$11 per PA), total system cost per PA decision is $35-$110.

## Reform trajectory

Multiple state laws + CMS-0057-F drive:
- Faster decision SLAs
- Gold-carding
- Greater FHIR PA API adoption
- Electronic submission requirements

## Agent guidance (UMAgent)

- Always cite AMA survey when answering "physician burden" or "patient harm" framings of PA.
- Don't quote single-statistic percentages without survey-year citation - data refreshes annually.
