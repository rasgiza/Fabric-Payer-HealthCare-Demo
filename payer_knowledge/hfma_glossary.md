# HFMA Revenue Cycle Glossary

Source: HFMA Revenue Cycle Outlook 2025 [CIT:HFMA-RCM-OUTLOOK-2025] + Healthcare Financial Management Association definitions.

## Core finance terms (CFOAgent)

| Term | Definition | Calculated as |
|---|---|---|
| **Initial denial rate** | First-pass denial rate | DeniedClaimCount / ClaimCount, both filtered to first submission |
| **Final denial rate** | Denied after appeals exhausted | terminal-denial claims / total claims |
| **Appeal overturn rate** | Reversed appealed denials / appealed denials | `[AppealOverturnRate]` |
| **AR days** | Days revenue outstanding | sum unpaid AR / avg daily charge |
| **First-pass resolution** | Paid on first submission, no rework | resolved-on-submit claims / total |
| **Rework cost per claim** | Industry $25-$118 range | manual + system + appeals time |
| **MLR** | Medical Loss Ratio | (incurred claims + QIA) / premium revenue |
| **MA MLR floor** | 85% federal floor on MA contracts | rebate triggers if MLR < 85% |
| **Commercial MLR floor** | 80% (small/individual) / 85% (large) per ACA |
| **PMPM** | Per Member Per Month | total $ / member-months |
| **PMPY** | Per Member Per Year | total $ / 12 / member-months |
| **Allowed amount** | Contracted-rate amount | NOT billed, NOT paid |
| **Billed amount** | Provider charge | usually well above allowed |
| **Paid amount** | Plan portion of allowed | allowed - member liability |
| **Member liability** | Copay + coinsurance + deductible portion |
| **Capitation** | Per-member fixed monthly payment regardless of utilization |
| **NSA-eligible claim** | OON emergency or facility-based subject to No Surprises Act IDR |

## Industry benchmarks (cite when narrating)

- Initial denial rate target: <5% (best in class), industry trending toward 15% [CIT:CHC-DENIAL-INDEX-2025]
- AR days: <50 days (best in class)
- First-pass resolution: >90% (best in class)
- MLR landing target: 82-87% MA, 80-85% Commercial
- Rework cost per claim: $25-$118 range [CIT:HFMA-RCM-OUTLOOK-2025]
