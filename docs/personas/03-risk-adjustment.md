# Persona 03 — Risk Adjustment / Actuarial Lead

## Snapshot

Owns the RAF score accuracy that drives MA payment. V28 phase-in is the single biggest revenue volatility item on the plan's books for 2026. Every undocumented chronic condition leaves money on the table; every unsupported diagnosis is a RADV/OIG audit risk.

## KPIs they own

| KPI | Target |
|---|---|
| Average RAF (by LOB / contract) | trend-up, V28-calibrated [CIT:CMS-HCC-V28-2026] |
| Suspect-code closure rate | ≥ 70% within fiscal year |
| RADV-style chart-review pass rate | ≥ 90% [CIT:OIG-MA-RA-AUDIT-2024] |
| Coding accuracy (sampled) | ≥ 95% |
| YTD-vs-prior-year diagnosis recapture | ≥ 85% |
| Improper-payment exposure | trend-down [CIT:GAO-MA-IMPROPER-2024] |

## Top 3 questions weekly

1. "Which members have historical HCCs not yet recaptured this year, ranked by RAF impact?"
2. "Show me suspect codes generated by the NLP pipeline this week, with confidence and supporting note excerpt."
3. "What's our V28 vs V24 blended RAF gap by contract, and how is the phase-in progressing?"

## Top 3 questions quarterly

1. "What's our projected revenue impact of V28 vs V24 by contract for PY2026 and PY2027?"
2. "Run a RADV-style spot audit on 100 random diagnoses this quarter — which HCCs failed support?"
3. "Compare our coder-captured HCCs vs claims-derived HCCs vs chart-review HCCs — where's the leakage?"
